The UCLA Campus Events Commission and the Benny family are proud to announce the presentation of the presentation of the 2011 Jack Benny Award to Betty White.

The UCLA Jack Benny Award was established in 1977 by the Campus Events Commission and Mary Benny, the late Jack Benny's wife, in order to recognize individuals for their unique contributions to comedy. Since its inception, the students of UCLA - with the approval Benny Estate - have presented the award to some of the foremost entertainers of our time.
